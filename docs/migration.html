Dashboard 1: State-wise Population Insights

This dashboard visualizes population distribution and growth trends across Australian states. Notably, New South Wales had the highest total resident population in 2016, while Queensland and Victoria also saw significant growth. Gender-based and age-group data reveal regional demographic differences crucial for planning services and resources.

Dashboard 2: Migrant Demographics by Country of Birth

This dashboard focuses on overseas-born residents, comparing age groups and gender across countries. India, China, and England emerge as the top countries of birth. Working-age population dominates most countries, making it a key segment for employment and education strategies.

Dashboard 3: Asia vs Europe Migrants and Settlement Patterns

Here, we explore the differences in migrant growth from Asia vs Europe, alongside the age profile of migrants and their preferred regions. Asian migration shows steeper growth compared to Europe. Also, New South Wales and Victoria remain the dominant settlement regions.
