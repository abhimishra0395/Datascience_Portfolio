Migration Trends in Australia

A comprehensive Power BI analysis exploring Australia’s population growth, migrant demographics, and settlement patterns from 1996 to 2016.

State-wise Population Trends

This dashboard explores regional population distribution and growth. New South Wales had the highest total population, while Queensland saw a steady rise. Gender and age segmentation revealed that the working force dominates all regions.

Migrant Demographics by Country of Birth

This dashboard visualises overseas-born populations by country, sex, and age group. England, China, and India are the top source countries. Most migrants belong to the working-age group, particularly males from China and England.

Asia vs Europe: Age Profiles & Regional Stories

This dashboard compares migrants from Asia and Europe. Asian migrants grew faster and settled mostly in Victoria. The overseas-born population showed younger age profiles compared to Australian-born individuals.
